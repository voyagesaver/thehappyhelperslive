Our Team

Hannah Stanke

Director of Communications

Hannah oversees our Communication and Content Marketing programs at Happy Helpers. She earned a B.A. in Government with a minor in. African Studies from Georgetown University, then continued her education at The Savannah College of Art and Design, where she was accepted to become a volunteer for The Peace Corps in South Africa. Hannah has 10+ years of teaching and tutoring experience and has worked for renowned non-profit organizations. Hannah is originally from New York City, and currently volunteers as an academic advisor and life coach to immigrants and students facing homelessness.

Brendalys Lebron

Learning Design Coordinator

Brenda Lebron is the Learning Design and Development Coordinator at Happy Helpers. She graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in 2018 with a BA in History and a certificate in Business Foundations from the McCombs School of Business. She is currently a master's student at the University of North Texas studying Learning Technologies, with a concentration in Instructional Systems Design.

Djeneba Diakite

Youth Advocacy Organizer

 Djeneba is a rising junior at Texas A&M University, College Station, Texas, pursuing a degree in Public Health. She aspires to become a Healthcare Administrator, as well as a Global Health Advocate. Djeneba joined the Happy Helpers team to fulfil her lifelong passion, to advocate for better opportunities for her peers. She believes that the Happy Helpers mission will enable youth to access a higher quality education, which in turns will allow them reach their full potential.
Djeneba is an active member of her global and local communities. She is involved in organizations such as TAMU UNICEF STUDENT ORGANIZATION at Texas A&M, Hopital Gabriel Toure Pediatrics, two orphanages in Mali, and many nonprofits in Houston.
